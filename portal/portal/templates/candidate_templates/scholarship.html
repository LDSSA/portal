In this batch of the Lisbon Data Science Starters Academy, we’ll have 10 seats for people who live with no / low income and therefore
can’t afford paying the normal fees (which are 250€ for student tickets, 500€ for regular tickets and 1500€ for company tickets).
The scholarship seats have a symbolic 20€ fee.

The process that we’ll use to decide whether to grant a scholarship is the following:

  • The candidate will start by reading these terms and conditions carefully, and confirm that they accept them.

  • The candidate will work on the admission tests.

  • If the candidate doesn't pass the admission tests, the admission process is over and they won’t be accepted in the LDSSA.

  • If the candidate passed the admission tests, and there are less than 10 scholarship candidates who passed the test, the candidate
    will automatically move on to the next phase. If there are more than 10 scholarship candidates who passed the test, the candidate will go
    through a random selection process to decide whether they will move on to the next phase. This random selection follows the same
    entrance type restrictions as the non-scholarship one.

  • If the candidate passed the selection phase, the next phase is a video call with a staff member of the Academy. The goal of the video call
    is for us to learn why the candidate needs a scholarship. It will be helpful for us to make the decision if the candidate has documentation
    that proves their no / low income situation, although this is not mandatory. This video call will be led by an interviewer who will collect
    the data that the candidate is comfortable sharing with us, by writing a report in a document shared with the candidate. At the end of
    the video call, the candidate will be asked to read the interviewer’s report and to sign it (by writing their full name at the bottom of
    the shared document). The actual decision will be made by the Academy’s executive team, based only on the content of the report.

  • In case the scholarship is granted, the candidate will have 48h to pay the 20€ fee and submit their payment proof. The payment proof will be
    validated by a staff member of the Academy, and in case it’s valid, the candidate is accepted in the batch of the LDSSA.

  • If a candidate doesn’t pass the interview phase or doesn't pay the fee, the next candidates in line will be called for the interview. So, even if
    a candidate is not called for a first round of interviews, they might still be called for a second or third one.

If less than 10 scholarships are granted, the remaining seats will be filled by non scholarship candidates.

If a candidate decides to apply for a scholarship at the beginning of the admission process, they won’t be able to change this at any time
during the process! If the scholarship is denied, they won’t be able to pay the normal fee and get in through the normal admissions funnel.
