Welcome to the admissions for the Lisbon Data Science Starters Academy!

Thank you for your interest in participating in batch4 of the LDSSA.
Before going anything else, please read all the details about the LDSSA here, about this admissions process here, and keep these main points in mind:

  • In order to get the most out of the experience, students should spend an average of 10h per week (and no less than 5h per week) studying on their own time. The Bootcamp and Hackathons are not included in this time commitment.
  • The Starters Academy will be remote this year. This means that, until further notice, all events are scheduled to happen remotely. In case it becomes safe, we may decide to have the later hackathons in person, but students may still join them remotely.
  • Since our Academy is based in Lisbon, all the Starter's Academy activities will happen during the day, Lisbon time. This includes the bootcamp, hackathons, and instructors' availability to answer questions on Slack. Students are required to be online during the bootcamp and hackathons. For students who are in very different timezones, this probably means that they'll have to join us at unusual hours, and that communicating with instructors will feel very async. So please take this in consideration when applying!
  • You can find all the relevant dates for the Starter's Academy on this calendar. The bootcamp and first hackathon are mandatory: if you miss them, you're not allowed to join any other activities in the course. For all the hackathons, in order to participate, you're required to have a minimum score of 80% on the 3 BLUs of that specialization.
  • At the end of the Starters Academy, we will emit a Data Science Resume that will describe your achievements. This resume will list all the Starters Academy's specializations, explain what it means to pass each specialization, and show the grades that you've achieved in each of them. If you miss a specialization, you'll score 0 (zero) on that specialization, and this will be shown in your resume. You'll only be issued a Data Science Resume in case you miss one or zero specializations. The Bootcamp days, Hackathon #1 and the Capstone cannot be missed or delivered late. If you can't attend one of the remaining specializations you're allowed to deliver all the evaluation elements for that specialization (BLUs + hackathon) until the end of the following month, and it won't count as a missed specialization for the resume.
  • Everyone is expected to comply with this Code of Conduct in all the interactions with the Lisbon Data Science Academy. This includes the candidates applying to the Starters Academy. During this admissions process, we'll ask you to read and acknowledge this Code of Conduct and you'll only be allowed to continue if you agree with it.
